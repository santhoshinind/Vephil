About Vephil

Vephil is a platform dedicated to developing and promoting Intrinsic Economic Philosophy (IEP) — a new economic framework that enables the creation of Artificial Economic Intelligence (AEI).

Our mission is to help individuals, organizations, and governments understand and apply economic principles in a relative, efficient, and harmonious way to build a smarter world.

Vision – AEI (Artificial Economic Intelligence)

With the development of AEI:

  • Global resources will be integrated and optimized efficiently
  • Economic agents will overcome limitations in resource utilization
  • Activities will be managed to their full potential
  • A Smart World will be created

In a Smart World:

  • 🌍 The United Nations achieves global peace
  • 🏛 Governments deliver good governance
  • 🏢 Firms achieve maximum returns
  • 👤 Individuals live authentic and fulfilled lives
